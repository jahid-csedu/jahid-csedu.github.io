NewzGator

Duration: Jul 2025 – Present

NewzGator is my personal project, designed and developed from scratch to serve as a modern news aggregation platform focusing exclusively on Bangladeshi newspapers. The platform collects, processes, and publishes the latest news articles across multiple categories including politics, business, sports, education, entertainment, technology, and lifestyle.

The project emphasizes real-time content updates, ensuring readers receive fresh news directly from verified Bangladeshi news sources. A key technical challenge was designing the system to efficiently crawl, process, and redirect news content while maintaining SEO-friendly URLs and ensuring compliance with Google News indexing standards.

Some of the major highlights of NewzGator include:

  • Automated Content Aggregation: Developed custom crawlers and ingestion pipelines to fetch articles from multiple Bangladeshi newspapers in near real-time.
  • Google News Integration: Implemented specialized news-sitemap.xml feeds with proper schema to enhance visibility on Google News and Top Stories.
  • Smart Categorization: Articles are dynamically categorized into 15+ segments (Politics, Sports, Education, Technology & Science, Business & Economy, etc.), improving navigation and discovery.
  • Performance & Scalability: Designed backend services with caching (Redis), asynchronous message queues (Kafka), and optimized database queries for handling thousands of articles efficiently.
  • SEO & Indexing: Implemented canonical tags, structured metadata, robots.txt, and sitemap index strategy to maximize search engine discoverability.
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for both mobile and desktop with a clean, user-friendly interface focused on content readability.

As a member of the development team, I worked on the backend development of the project — from system design, backend development, database design, and data collection using crawling and scrapping, data cleaning, deployment and monitoring using serverless architecture. The platform is currently live at https://www.newzgator.com.

Technologies used: Python, BERT Classifiers, AWS Lambda, S3, DynamoDB, CloudWatch, AWS API Gateway, Google Search Console
